Strategic Alliance

Global Alliance for Vaccine Innovation

ABSNOVACELL, in partnership with ABS Inc. USA, is advancing the proprietary NOVACELL™ Vaccine Platform Technology — a next-generation system designed to produce highly efficacious, scalable and cost-effective vaccines with duarability.

Current platforms often introduce mutations in the HA region, leading to reduced efficacy. In contrast, AI/ML integrated with NOVACELL offer precise immunogenicity.

This global vaccine alliance integrates cutting-edge process science, GMP manufacturing and regulatory excellence to support the rapid and affordable delivery of vaccines to both developed and emerging markets.

Together, we are redefining vaccine accessibility through technology transfer, local production, regional clinical studies and collaborative global supply chains.

Advanced Therapeutics

Gene Therapy Partnerships

Expanding from vaccines to advanced therapeutics, ABSNOVACELL is in active collaboration and negotiation with the U.S. companies, Saudi Arabia companies with middile east and Asian companies to deploy its proprietary Gene Therapy, Gene Editing and Advanced Biologics Platforms.

These partnerships focus on Next-gen AAV-based gene therapy, gene editing for rare disease treatment and technology transfer initiatives that bring curative solutions closer to patients.

Our goal: to make gene therapy accessible, affordable and sustainable, empowering new regional centers of excellence in regenerative medicine.
