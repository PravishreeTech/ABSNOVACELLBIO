Pioneering Biologics for a Healthier Tomorrow

We are dedicated to discovering, developing and delivering transformative biologic, biosimilar and cell & gene therapies that address critical unmet medical needs cost effectively.

Explore Our Pipeline Our Mission

ABSNovacell Biopharmaceuticals Limited is promoted by world-renowned scientists, business, technology, finance and management professionals with significant experience and investments. The Board of Directors, Senior Management and Scientific and Technical Advisory Board consist of global leaders who represent world-class biopharmaceutical companies and academicians. Their purpose is to commercialize established novel R&D pipeline products (new technology platform) with current Good Manufacturing Practices (cGMP) as per highly regulated standards. Its scientific and financial advisors are world-renowned and advise the company on strategy and execution.

Strategic Vision

  • To become the global leader in intelligent biologic and small-molecule repurposing, bridging data science, Artificial Intelligence / Machine Learning, molecular engineering and translational medicine.
  • Focused on rapid, cost-efficient innovation for rare, infectious and degenerative diseases through computational insight and bench-to-bed translation.
  • Cell, Gene Therapy and Gene editing and universal vaccines.
Mission
To be amongst the leading biopharma companies in the world by extending our presence globally through quality and cost-effective biologics, biotherapeutics and biosimilars to compassionately address human and animal suffering.
Vision
By leveraging extensive biotech expertise, we envision creating innovative biologics, speciality pharmaceuticals and biosimilars businesses globally.
Goal
We want to leverage our experience in novel biologics and biosimilars to become unparalleled leaders in the world.
