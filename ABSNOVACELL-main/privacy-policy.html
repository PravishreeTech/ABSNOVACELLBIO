Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

  • ABSNOVACELL (“ABSNOVACELL,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy of website visitors, clients and service users.
  • This policy applies when we act as a data controller for your personal data: we determine purposes and means of processing such data.
  • We use cookies to enhance your experience. Where not strictly necessary, consent will be requested on first visit.
  • "We," "us," and "our" refer to ABSNOVACELL. See Section 15 for contact details.

2. How We Use Your Personal Data

  • We may process these categories of personal data:
    • Usage data: IP address, browser, referral source, visit duration, navigation paths, interaction data.
    • Enquiry data: information from contact forms or service inquiries.
    • Notification data: newsletter or event registration info.
    • Correspondence data: communications sent via email, phone, or site forms.
  • Usage data may be collected via Google Analytics, LinkedIn Insights, or similar.
  • Data is processed to provide services, respond to inquiries, send updates, analyze site use and maintain business records.
  • Legal basis: Your consent, contract performance, legal compliance, or our legitimate interests.

3. Providing Your Personal Data to Others

  • We may share personal data with trusted third parties supporting our operations (e.g., hosting, analytics) as needed.
  • Data may be disclosed if required by law, legal proceedings, or to protect ABSNOVACELL, its employees, or clients.

4. International Transfers

  • Based in the United States, ABSNOVACELL’s website is accessible worldwide.
  • If data is transferred outside your region, appropriate safeguards will be applied per applicable laws.

5. Retaining and Deleting Data

  • We retain data only as long as needed for stated purposes.
  • Typically, enquiry, correspondence and notification data are retained up to five years or as legally required.
  • At retention end, data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

6. Amendments

  • We may update this policy from time to time.
  • Check this page periodically; major updates may be notified via email or on the website.

7. Your Rights

  • You have rights under applicable law:
    • Access your data
    • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data
    • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
    • Restrict or object to processing
    • Data portability
    • Withdraw consent any time (if based on consent)
    • Complain to supervisory authority
  • To exercise these, contact us (see Section 15).

8. Third-Party Websites

  • Links may lead to third-party sites: ABSNOVACELL is not responsible for their privacy policies or content.

9. Personal Data of Children

  • Our site and services are not directed at anyone under 18.
  • If we learn we hold data for a child under 18, it will be promptly deleted.

10. Updating Your Information

  • Please notify us if personal data we hold should be corrected or updated.

11. About Cookies

  • Cookies are used to store preferences, analyze usage and support functionality.
  • Cookies may be session-based (expire after browser session) or persistent (remain until deleted).
  • Cookies typically do not identify you personally but may be linked to other data.

12. Cookies We Use

  • ABSNOVACELL may use cookies for:
    • Analytics: Google Analytics, LinkedIn Insights

  • For Google’s privacy policy, visit: Google Privacy Policy

14. Managing Cookies
